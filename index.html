Insights English Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Explore the sections below to understand how we handle information.

This section outlines our fundamental approach to your privacy. We've built "Insights English" from the ground up to be a safe learning environment that respects your personal space and data.

  • No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) Collected: We do not ask for, collect, store, or process any information that can directly identify you. This includes your name, email, phone number, location, photos, or contacts.
  • No User Accounts: The app is fully functional without a login system. You can start learning immediately without creating an account or providing any personal credentials.

To provide you with a functional and up-to-date learning experience, our app uses certain data and services. This section clarifies what data is used and for what purpose, ensuring transparency about how the app operates.

Grammar Content Data & Firestore

The core educational content (grammar rules, examples, exercises) is stored in Google Cloud Firestore. This is a secure database used only to deliver and update the app's content. It does not store any of your personal data or interaction history.

Internet Connection

The app requires an internet connection to fetch the latest content from Firestore. This connection is exclusively for content delivery and is not used to collect any information from your device.

Log Data for Error Reporting

In the event of an app error, we may collect anonymous technical information called Log Data (e.g., device type, OS version, IP address) through services like Google Play Services. This data is vital for us to identify and fix bugs, ensuring the app runs smoothly. It is not linked to you personally.

We use third-party services to support the app's functionality and to keep it free for everyone. This section details these services and explains their role, particularly regarding advertising.

Advertising with Google AdMob

To support the app, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and other technologies to collect data and serve ads that are relevant to you. We do not control the data collected by AdMob. For more information, you can review Google's Privacy Policy.

Other Service Providers

In addition to AdMob and Firestore, the app relies on Google Play Services and other Firebase SDKs for essential functionalities like error reporting and ensuring the app works correctly on Android devices. These third parties are obligated not to use any data for purposes other than providing these services.

We are committed to protecting our users, especially younger learners. This section covers our approach to data security and our policy regarding children's privacy.

Data Security

While we do not collect personal data, we take the security of the app's operational data seriously. We use commercially acceptable means to protect information. However, please remember that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

"Insights English" is designed for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13, as we do not collect this type of information from any user. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with any information, please contact us so we can take necessary action.
